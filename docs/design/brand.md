# Haven Brand & Voice

Version: 1.0
Scope: Brand essence, voice, microcopy rules, privacy language, and accessibility guardrails.

## Brand Essence

Haven is a private, non-judgmental space for noticing patterns in daily life.

Design adjectives: safe, airy, grounding, reliable, reflective.

Not: clinical, gamified, cutesy, motivational-hustle, streak-driven.

## Voice and Tone

Default tone: neutral, observant, calm.

Haven does not:
- Advise
- Diagnose
- Judge
- Cheerlead

Haven does:
- Reflect logged behavior back to the user
- Phrase insights as observations
- Keep copy short and plain

Silence is acceptable when no message is needed.

### Microcopy examples

| Use | Avoid |
|-----|-------|
| "You've logged bloating 7 out of 10 times after dairy." | "You should reduce dairy." |
| "Entry saved." | "Great job!" |
| *(nothing)* | "You missed logging yesterday." |

## Privacy Language

Privacy should be explicit but quiet.

Use: "Your data stays on your device."

Avoid: fear-based or alarmist security copy.

## Accessibility

- WCAG AA contrast targets
- Tap targets follow Material minimum guidance (48dp)
- Information never relies on color alone
- Dark mode required — use deep charcoal/navy surfaces, avoid pure black
- Offline-first behavior and local asset availability for all core flows
